Significance of the Project

This project focuses on Turkey as a case study to analyze the spatial distribution of air pollution and its relationship with land cover types and population exposure.

By integrating high-resolution CAMS atmospheric data, ESA CCI land cover maps, and WorldPop population grids, the study offers a detailed spatial understanding of environmental health risks in the region.

The interactive WebGIS platform developed in this project enables users to visualize pollutant concentrations, land use patterns, and population vulnerability, making it a powerful tool for policymakers, researchers, and the public.
